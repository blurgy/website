Latin Adverbs

Many Latin adverbs are formed from Latin adjectives:

First-Second Declension

To form an adverb from a first-second declension adjective, append the ending -e_ to the stem of the adjective, e.g. pulcher, pulchra, pulchrum (beautiful) becomes pulchre_ (beautifully). Note that many first-second declension adjectives have corresponding adverbs that are irregularly formed. Adverbs are usually placed directly before the words the modify.
